Publications

I am a researcher at the DFKI RIC and I currently work in the project APRIL. Previously I worked in TransFIT, COROMA, and InFuse. At the moment my scientific work focuses on imitation learning and reinforcement learning. Before joining DFKI I worked at the Robotics Research Group of the University of Bremen in the project BesMan. As a student I participated in the RoboCup SPL team B-Human.

I wrote my diploma thesis (German) in the field of artificial neural networks and my dissertation about learning behaviors for robots.

You can also find an overview of my publications at Google scholar.
